EPOCH Workshop 2026

The workshop will consist of a mixture of talks and practical sessions. In the practical session you will be encouraged to work on a ‘project’ of your choice and of interest to your research. Demonstrators will be on hand to help with problem setup and answer questions. The workshop has no registration fee but you need to register here.
